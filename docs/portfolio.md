## LinkedIn post (2–3 sentences)
As a Lean Six Sigma trainer, I wanted to know whether an AI could grade a learner's
analysis the way I do — grounded in real methodology, not vibes. So I built DMAIC
Coach: it guides you through the Measure phase, role-plays a sceptical stakeholder,
and then *evaluates your work* against a rubric — verifying every number itself over
MCP and citing the methodology behind each score. Runs in under five minutes with no
API key (deterministic mock provider), and the LLM is swappable (Claude/GPT/Grok).
